Shopping Centers Featuring Anime Characters

Akihabara - The Anime Mecca

Known as the world's largest anime and electronics hub, Akihabara offers countless shops specializing in anime merchandise, figures, cosplay, and more.

Osaka Nipponbashi (Den Den Town)

Popular for its extensive collection of manga, anime goods, and collectibles, Nipponbashi is a must-visit for otakus.

Ikebukuro - Otome Road

Focused mainly on female anime fans, Otome Road offers an abundance of anime shops, themed cafes, and events.
